> During my internship in the First Cloud Journey program, I had the opportunity to explore various AWS technologies, participate in workshops, work on a real-world project, and receive valuable guidance from mentors and the FCJ team. Below are my personal reflections and suggestions based on this experience.

### General Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**

The working environment was professional, friendly, and highly supportive. Everyone was willing to share their knowledge and assist whenever I encountered technical challenges. The collaborative atmosphere encouraged continuous learning and made it easy to communicate with mentors and teammates throughout the internship.

**2. Mentor and Team Support**

My mentor provided clear guidance and encouraged independent problem-solving rather than simply providing answers. This approach helped me improve my analytical thinking and technical skills. The FCJ team was also responsive in providing learning materials, organizing workshops, and supporting administrative matters throughout the program.

**3. Relevance to My Academic Background**

The internship was closely aligned with my Data Science major. It allowed me to apply concepts related to data processing, data engineering, and analytics while gaining practical experience with AWS cloud services through a real-world project.

**4. Learning and Skill Development Opportunities**

The program provided excellent opportunities to learn modern cloud technologies through hands-on practice. Throughout the internship, I gained practical experience with Amazon S3, AWS Glue, Amazon Athena, Amazon EC2, AWS Lambda, Amazon Kinesis, and Streamlit. In addition, I improved my problem-solving, self-learning, technical documentation, and project implementation skills.

**5. Company Culture and Teamwork**

The team maintained a positive and collaborative working culture where everyone respected and supported one another. Open communication and knowledge sharing created a comfortable environment that encouraged learning and continuous improvement.

**6. Internship Policy and Benefits**

The program offered valuable learning resources, technical workshops, and real project experience. Instead of focusing only on theory, interns had the opportunity to build an end-to-end cloud solution, which significantly enhanced practical knowledge and technical confidence.

---

### Additional Questions

**What was your favorite part of the internship?**

The most rewarding part was building a complete AWS Data Lakehouse solution from scratch. From designing the architecture and implementing the data pipeline to developing the dashboard and deploying it on Amazon EC2, every stage provided valuable hands-on experience and a deeper understanding of cloud-based data engineering.

**What do you think could be improved for future interns?**

It would be beneficial to organize more technical sharing sessions on real-world AWS architectures, FinOps best practices, and project review meetings. Regular feedback sessions would also help interns improve their projects throughout the internship.

**Would you recommend the First Cloud Journey program to your friends? Why?**

Yes. I would definitely recommend this program because it provides both theoretical knowledge and practical experience with modern cloud technologies. Participants have the opportunity to build real-world projects while receiving guidance from experienced mentors, making it an excellent learning environment for students interested in cloud computing and data engineering.

---

### Suggestions and Expectations

**Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?**

I would like to see more advanced workshops covering topics such as Data Engineering, DevOps, cloud architecture design, and AWS cost optimization (FinOps). In addition, more project review sessions throughout the internship would help interns receive timely feedback and improve the quality of their work.

**Would you like to continue participating in this program in the future?**

Yes. If given the opportunity, I would be happy to participate in future advanced programs or real-world cloud projects to further strengthen my knowledge of Cloud Computing, Data Engineering, and Machine Learning on AWS.

**Additional Comments**

I would like to sincerely thank my mentor, the FCJ team, and everyone involved in the First Cloud Journey program for providing such a valuable learning experience. This internship has significantly improved both my technical expertise and my professional skills. I hope the program will continue to grow and provide the same opportunities for future interns.
